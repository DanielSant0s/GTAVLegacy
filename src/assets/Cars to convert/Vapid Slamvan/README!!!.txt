DESCRIPTION:

Original GTA SA slamvan, adapted to its factory version, similar to the GTA V slamvan.

* VAPID logo from GTA V
* Stepside box
* Classic Wheels
* Lifted suspension
* New Grill (Low Poly Version of the GTA EPFLC Slamvan)
* Paintable in 3 colors:
  1: Middle and lower part
  2: Top, Inner box, Wheel rim.
  3: Chrome Details, Valve Caps and Interior Reflections
* Added Front and Rear Bumper
* It has as extras: Canvas in the Box, Roll Bar, Spare Wheels, Stakes and Visor, being able to appear in 6 different combinations of these
* Contains 4 Paint Jobs From GTA V
  1) Blue Flames
  2) Rusty Flames
  3) Blacktop Bomber
  4) Black Striped


* All Accessories used are Low or Mid Poly, that is, they fit perfectly in the Game Environment (they are SA Style)
