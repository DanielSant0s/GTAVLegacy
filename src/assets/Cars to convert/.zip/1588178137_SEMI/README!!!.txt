DESCRIPTION:

Original BARRACKS OL of GTA SA, adapted to Semi Truck like the Barracks Semi from GTA V.

* MTL, FLATBED and ZANCUDO logos from GTA V
* Camouflage paint scheme
* Lifted suspension, larger wheels and Dually wheels in the rear
* Full front adapted to a reverse hood (As in the Versions, GTA V and real life)
  - Added rear-view mirrors and kills to the bonnet.
* Fully repaintable with 2-tone detailing
* Appears with or without  roof
